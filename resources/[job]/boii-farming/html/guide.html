Farming



Handbook
Planting



Before you can start planting crops you first need to aquire yourself some equipment.

You will need some seeds, watering cans, and fertilizer to ensure your crops grow to their full potential!

All equipment you require can be purchased from Shady Hills Farms, just talk to a farmer they will help you out.

Once you all the equipment you need, find yourself a nice spot of fertile land and start planting.

After you have planted your first load of crops make sure to check on them periodically and give them a little water and fertilizer.

After some time passes you will be able to harvest your crops ready to use or take to the packing station and prepare for sale!
Picking Fruit



Shady Hills Farms has plenty of fruit ready for picking all year round!

Check your city map and head over to either of our fruit tree patches.

Here you can pick yourself some fresh apples, oranges, lemons or limes!

Doing this is simple. Just reach up into the trees and pick fresh fruits to your hearts content!

Once you have collected a bunch of fresh fruit, you can head back over to the packing station to pack your fruit into crates ready for selling!

Be careful you dont waste your time by collecting any unripe fruit...
Animals



Cows

Our dairy cows provide some of the freshest milk throughout Los Santos.

To collect yourself some milk you first need to pick up some milking pails. You can find these next to the local Farmer.

Once you have your milking pails in hand head over to the cows and start milking.

Chickens

Looking for a good source of protein? Collecting our eggs couldnt be easier!

Head over to the chicken pen and take a look under some chickens and see if they have layed any eggs.

Pigs


Pigs are friendly right? Of course!

If you are looking for a way to destress after a hard days working on the farm head over to our pig pen and pet your favourite pig!


Dogs

We have a couple of dogs here at the farm.

If you need a break from working take a load off and pet one of them! Petting a dog can be great for relieving that stress after a hard days work!


Troughs

Dont forget to refill the feeding troughs!

Grab yourself a haybale and head over to any of the troughs around the farm.

You will find our hay bale storage near our Farmer.
Packing Crops



Now you have gathered yourself a nice selection of crops you can make use of our packing stations!

To pack your goods ready for selling you will need to grab yourself some empty sacks or fruit crates. Depending on what you wish to pack.

We keep a storage of sacks and crates outside. You can find these next to the Farmer.

Once you have some sacks or fruit crates head over to the packing stations and start packing your goods!

After you finish up with your packing, you are ready to earn yourself some money after a long days work!

Selling Crops



Now that you have packed your crops you can finally get paid for all that work!

Load up your goods and take a trip down to Frigit and speak to the warehouse worker out front.

They will give you a good price for an honest days work.

If you need to you can also purchase yourself some fresh produce whilst you are there, provided it is in stock!
